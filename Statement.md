📌 Problem Statement

As students, we often notice that our productivity is not the same every day. Some days we feel active and focused, while on other days we struggle to study, even if we spend the same amount of time.
This difference usually happens because of factors like our mood and sleep, but we don’t really track or understand how these things affect our performance.
Right now, there is no simple tool that helps students connect these daily habits with their productivity in an easy way.
So, this project is designed to solve that problem by:

- Tracking mood, study hours, and sleep hours
- Calculating a productivity score
- Giving small and helpful suggestions
- Showing patterns using graphs
- Predicting productivity using a basic machine learning model

The main idea behind this project is to help students understand their daily routine better and improve their productivity step by step in a simple and practical way.
